Soltis’ Hope Animal Rescue is a USA nonprofit organization that helps rescue stray and

abused dogs found on the streets of Far Western Nepal. This organization provides

shelter, food, and a safe home for these dogs. Soltis’ Hope Animal Rescue website aims

to raise awareness about what is going on with these animals and prevent it from

continuing.
